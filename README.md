# Credit Sentinel
AI-Driven Credit Risk Intelligence Platform

Early development. Credit Sentinel is currently in the concept and early-build stage. This README describes the vision and direction — features and structure will evolve as development progresses.

What is Credit Sentinel?
Credit Sentinel is an AI-powered platform that assesses and monitors credit risk — helping lenders and financial teams make faster, more accurate, and more consistent lending decisions.
Traditional credit assessment is slow, manual, and often relies on limited or outdated signals. Credit Sentinel aims to change that by applying machine learning to borrower and transaction data, producing clear risk scores and early warnings.
A Skillvance Technologies product.

The Problem
Credit decisions are still heavily manual and inconsistent. Lenders struggle to assess risk accurately at scale, leading to bad loans, missed good borrowers, and slow turnaround. Early signs of rising risk often go unnoticed until it's too late.

The Vision
Credit Sentinel is being built to provide:

Credit risk scoring — generate clear, data-driven risk scores for borrowers.
Early-warning detection — flag rising risk and potential defaults before they happen.
Explainable decisions — surface the key factors behind each score, not just a number.
Portfolio monitoring — track risk across an entire book of borrowers in one place.
Dashboard & insights — make risk intelligence accessible and actionable.
