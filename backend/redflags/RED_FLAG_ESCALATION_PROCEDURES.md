# Red Flag Escalation Procedures

## Purpose

This document defines the escalation workflow for applications identified as high risk.

---

# Escalation Workflow

Application Submitted

↓

Red Flag Detection

↓

Analyst Review

↓

Decision

• Approve

• Request Additional Information

• Escalate

---

# Escalation Rules

Senior Analyst Review

Required when:

- Payment History Issues
- Low Credit Score

Risk Committee Review

Required when:

- More than two Red Flags are triggered.

---

# Escalation Process

The analyst selects:

"Escalate for Review"

Reason options include:

- Manual Review Required
- Policy Exception
- Additional Verification
- High Credit Risk

Every escalation is recorded within the audit log.

---

# Service Level Agreement

Senior Analyst

Review completed within:

24 Hours

Risk Committee

Review completed within:

2 Business Days

---

# Applicant Communication

If escalation delays the lending decision, the applicant is notified through the customer communication process.

---

# Audit Requirements

Every escalation records:

- Application ID
- Analyst Name
- Timestamp
- Escalation Reason
- Assigned Reviewer
- Final Outcome
