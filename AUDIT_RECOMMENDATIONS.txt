AUDIT RECOMMENDATIONS SYSTEM

Week 7 Day 2 – Decision Audit Recommendations

## Objective

Analyze analyst decision behavior and generate automated recommendations to improve decision consistency, compliance, and operational efficiency.

---

## Recommendation Logic

### Approval Rate Analysis

Rule 1:
IF approval rate > 90%

Recommendation:
"May be too lenient. Review decision thresholds and verify rejection criteria are being applied consistently."

Severity:
Medium

---

Rule 2:
IF approval rate < 50%

Recommendation:
"May be too strict. Review rejected applications and verify approvals are not being unnecessarily declined."

Severity:
Medium

---

### Decision Latency Analysis

Rule 3:
IF average decision latency > 10 minutes

Recommendation:
"May require workflow optimization or additional training."

Severity:
Low

---

### Rejection Pattern Analysis

Rule 4:
IF analyst has zero rejections

Recommendation:
"May not be applying decision rules consistently. Conduct audit review."

Severity:
High

---

### Consistency Analysis

Rule 5:
IF decision consistency score < 85%

Recommendation:
"Review decision rationale and provide rule refresher training."

Severity:
Medium

---

## Proposed API Design

Endpoint:

/api/audit/analyst-performance

Response Example:

{

"approval_rate": 82,
"average_latency_minutes": 6,
"consistency_score": 94,
"recommendation": "None",

}

Returned Fields:

* Analyst Name

* Approval Rate

* Average Decision Latency
