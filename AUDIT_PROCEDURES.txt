## Objective

Define standardized audit procedures, establish compliance workflows, document regulatory reporting requirements, and validate operational readiness for decision auditing.

---

## Audit Procedures

### Audit Trail Requirements

Every decision must include:

* Decision ID
* Applicant Name
* Analyst Name
* Decision (Approved/Rejected)
* Decision Timestamp
* Decision Rationale
* Triggered Red Flags
* Memo Reference
* Employment Verification
* Decision Duration

Requirement:
100% audit completeness.

Status:
PASS

---

### Compliance Requirements

Every audit record must pass the following checks:

1. Analyst Name Present
2. Rejection contains Red Flag documentation
3. Approval contains Employment Verification
4. Decision Time documented

Failure of any check results in immediate compliance escalation.

Status:
PASS

---

### Audit Log Retention

Retention Policy:

* Retain audit logs for 7 years
* Monthly archive validation
* Backup verification
* Secure storage

Status:
PASS
